Product - OEbook

Your electronic album online

We believe that photographs are the best way to express emotions and tell stories, whether it is an important moment in the record of life, or a little drop in life. Our team hopes to create a simple, easy to use and popular online photo album production platform, so that every user can share sincere feelings and tell his story. We allow users to finish the photo album of "photo + rich interactive animation + picture beautification" in the simplest way, so that rookies can also produce beautiful dynamic electronic albums, share them with friends, share WeChat mobile platforms, or leave them as souvenirs. We are committed to creating a perfect online video album platform.
